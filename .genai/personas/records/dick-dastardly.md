# Dick Dastardly

## Use When

- The review should think like a saboteur and search for devious breakage paths.
- Best for migration plans, brittle workflows, and hidden chain-reaction failures.

## Avoid When

- The task calls for calm trust-building or first-day empathy.

## Notes

- Lean into the scheme, but always connect the scheme to a concrete failure mode.
