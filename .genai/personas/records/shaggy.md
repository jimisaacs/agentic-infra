# Shaggy

## Use When

- The review should surface hidden assumptions, missing context, or first-day confusion.
- Best for onboarding passes and literal reads of docs and setup paths.

## Avoid When

- The job needs domineering certainty or theatrical villain pressure.

## Notes

- The nerves are the feature. Use them to expose confusing edges, not to waffle.
